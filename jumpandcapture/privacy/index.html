Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Jump & Capture (“the App”) is published by Maverick Codebase. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles when you play on iOS or Android.

Information we collect

  • Your display name and character portrait.
  • Game progress and stats stored on your device (wins, XP, scenarios, streaks, and saved matches).
  • Settings such as sound, music, haptics, and whether play reminders are on.

How we use it

  • To run the game and show your profile, stats, and progress.
  • To send optional local play reminders if you turn notifications on.
  • To improve the App using general usage and crash information.

Playing with others

  • In Same Wi‑Fi or online matches, your display name and character may be shown to your opponent.
  • Same Wi‑Fi matches stay between nearby devices.
  • Online play uses Apple Game Center on iOS and Google Play Games on Android to sign in. Matches may include players on either platform.
  • The leaderboard may show your display name, character, and lifetime XP.

Ads & purchases

  • The free version shows ads from Google. You can buy Remove Ads to turn them off.
  • On iOS, when the system asks for tracking permission, you can allow or decline. Declining still shows ads, but they are not personalized.
  • Purchases are handled by Apple (iOS) or Google Play (Android). We only learn whether Remove Ads is active — not your payment details.

Your choices

  • You can change your name and character anytime in the App.
  • You can adjust sound, music, haptics, and notifications in Settings.
  • You can restore Remove Ads on a new device with Restore Purchases.
  • Deleting the App removes locally stored data from your device.

Children

  • The App is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
  • The “Last updated” date at the top of this page shows when it was revised.

Contact

  • Questions about privacy can be sent to maverickcodebase@gmail.com.
